## Amazon-Sales-Performance-Dashboard-using-Power-BI

Project Overview 
Developed a Power BI sales analytics dashboard that transformed raw transaction data into actionable business insights. Analyzed sales, profit, customer activity, product performance, and geographic trends using Power Query, DAX, and interactive visualizations to support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Insights
 * **Overall Business Performance:** The analysis shows total sales of 695.79K and a total profit of 104.24K, resulting in a profit margin of 0.15.
 * **Volume Metrics:** The business has processed 3,043 total orders with a cumulative quantity of 12K items sold.
 * **Operational Efficiency:** The average order value is 228.65.
 * **Sales Distribution:** Insights are derived from "Total Sales by Product Name" and "Total Sales by Geography," which serve as key drivers for understanding market reach and product demand.
 * **Customer Engagement:** The "Customer Sales by EmailID" visualization highlights sales contributions per customer, identifying top-performing accounts.
## Tools Used
 * **Visualization:** Power BI (used for creating interactive dashboards and reports).
 * **Data Analysis:** SQL (used for data cleaning, extraction, and manipulation).
 * **Dataset:** Available in the dataset/ folder as amazon_sales.csv.
