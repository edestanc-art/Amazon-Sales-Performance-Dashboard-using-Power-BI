# MAZON SALES PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD

# Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard developed using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and geographical sales distribution. The objective is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying key revenue drivers, profitable products, high-value customers, and market opportunities.

# Business Problem
Organizations need visibility into sales trends, profitability, customer purchasing patterns, and product performance to make informed business decisions. This project analyzes Amazon sales data to answer critical business questions such as:
* How much revenue was generated?
* Which products contribute the most sales and profit?
* Who are the top customers?
* How geographic regions perform best? * Power do sales change over time?

# Key Insights
1. Strong Revenue Performance
The business generated approximately 695.79K in sales revenue from over 3,000 customer orders.
2. Healthy Profitability
A total profit of 104.24K was achieved, resulting in a profit margin of approximately 15%.
3. Revenue Concentration Among Top Products
A small group of products contributes a significant portion of total revenue, indicating opportunities for focused inventory planning and marketing.
4. Customer Value Distribution
Top customers generate substantially higher sales than the average customer, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies.
5. Geographic Revenue Variation
Sales performance differs significantly across regions, suggesting opportunities for targeted regional growth initiatives.
6. Product Profitability Differences
Some products generate high sales but comparatively lower profits, indicating potential pricing or cost optimization opportunities.
# Recommendations
Inventory Optimization
Prioritize stock management for top-performing products to prevent stockouts and maximize revenue.
Customer Retention
Develop loyalty and retention programs targeted at high-value customers.
Profitability Improvement
Review products with high sales but low profit contribution to improve margins.
Regional Expansion
Increase marketing and sales efforts in underperforming regions with growth potential.
Product Portfolio Strategy
Promote highly profitable products through targeted campaigns to increase overall Profitability.
